About Us

Raj Riddhi Group: Quality Packaging Solutions

Established in 2007, Raj Packaging is a leader in the woven fabric packaging industry. Based in Kadapa, India, we specialize in manufacturing HDPE and PP woven fabrics and a wide range of packaging materials like sacks, tarpaulins, and jumbo bags. Our strong production capacity allows us to manufacture up to 40 million bags annually.
