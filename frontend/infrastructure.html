Our Infrastructure

Advanced Manufacturing Facilities

At Raj Riddhi Group, our advanced manufacturing facilities, featuring high-capacity extrusion lines, automated looms, and in-house Rotogravure printing units, ensure precision, efficiency, and consistent product quality. This state-of-the-art infrastructure enables us to meet large-scale production demands and maintain industry-leading standards.

Lohia Tape Plant

The Lohia Tape Plant produces high-quality tapes for woven fabrics with stringent quality control to ensure durability and reliability.

Gusset and Semi-Automatic Cutting & Stitching Machines

These machines ensure precision in cuts and stitches for custom-sized products, improving efficiency without compromising quality.

Circular Looms

Our circular looms create high-strength, flexible fabrics, ensuring the consistent production of durable woven materials.

Tensile Strength Testing Machine

This machine tests the strength and durability of materials, ensuring that our products meet industry standards and customer expectations.

JP and Sunderlam Make Extrusion Coating Lines

These coating lines apply uniform coatings for enhanced strength and water resistance, supporting high-volume production of laminated fabrics.

High-Speed 8 Color Rotogravure Printing Machine

This machine offers unmatched printing accuracy and vibrant colors for complex designs, ensuring top-tier print quality for large orders.
